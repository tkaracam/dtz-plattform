# Store Copy – English (Final Draft)

**App Name:** DTZ-LID edu
**Short Description:** DTZ A2–B1 training: listening, reading, writing, corrections.

**Long Description:**
DTZ-LID edu is a training platform for the German Test for Immigrants (A2–B1).
Practice listening, reading and writing with exam-style tasks and see corrected letters in the portal.

Features:
- DTZ Listening Part 1–4
- DTZ Reading Part 1–5
- Writing & Upload
- Corrected letters in the portal

Note: Content is exam-style but not official exam material.

**Keywords:**
DTZ, German, A2, B1, listening, reading, writing, exam, training
